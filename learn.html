Learning Pedagogy

The title may seem a little contradictory, but I try to keep the student in mind whenever teaching something. What other reason would you be teaching for, if not to better the student?

We should all act as learners, keeping a curious and inquisitive mindset. Reduce yourself to an amateur and invite the "stupid" questions. We all started somewhere, but if Little Johnny wasn't paying attention, then give him detention.
